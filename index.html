Hello there!

I am a graduate student at the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) where I am pursuing the Masters of Science in Computer Science degree with a Machine Learning specialization. I am also a part of the Pathology Dynamics Lab at Georgia Tech where I am working on Biomedical Information Extraction using NLP techniques under the guidance of Prof. Cassie Mitchell.

I previously completed my undergraduate studies at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay where I majored in Mechanical Engineering and completed dual minor degrees in the Department of Computer Science and the Centre of Machine Intelligence and Data Science.

During my undergraduate studies, I was involved in research at InfoLab, IIT Bombay where I was fortunate to work under the guidance of wonderful mentors like Prof. Soumen Chakrabarti and Prof. Abir De. I did my Undergraduate Thesis on Joint Completion and Alignment of Multilingual Knowledge Graphs with the guidance of Prof. Chakrabarti.

I intend to use this website to give the world a peek into my mind and to share updates about my work.
